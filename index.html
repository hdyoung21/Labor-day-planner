    • Kirksville

    Kirksville is a small city about 40 minutes north of the lodge that would be a good option for getting picked up from the airport or ubering. The flight is gennerally another $25-50 than KC or STL but 2 hours closer. Uber is available up there but its hit or miss. Depending on number of people trying to fly into Kirksville we may rent a van.

    Kansas City

    Flying into Kansas City puts you about 2 1/2 hours away from the lodge with a few riverboat casinos on the way. If you dont want to pay for a rental the entire weekend look at dropping off your vehicle in kirksville the following day or plan with others to rent a van. Enterprise would be my suggestion if wanting to drop car.

    St. Louis

    Flying into St. Louis leaves about a 3 hour drive to the Lodge. Enterprise is suggested if you would like to rent a car just for the days you are traveling as they have a drop off near by.

    Drive

    The Lodge is about a 750 mile or 12 hour drive from Atlanta. Carpooling is suggested and if you dont have anyone you are planning to ride with let me know and ill connect you with others.
